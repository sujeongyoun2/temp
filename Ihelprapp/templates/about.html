About Q&A

Welcome! Learn more about our services

Why do you need our service?

In this day and age, the number of dual-income households is increasing, and many parents are seeking childcare services. Parents want a guardian whom they can trust. So, they either ask around to find someone within the circle of people that they know or find one through agencies. These methods require unnecessary time and effort if the preferences between the guardian and parents are not met. Especially, finding a babysitter through an agency involves the mediation of every conversation between parents and caregivers, which makes it time-consuming

What do we provide?

  • Parents can choose a sitter from a larger pool of people with few mouse clicks
  • A grading system and review will ensure the credibility of the employee
  • The list of job hunting posts can be filtered by conditions provided in each post, and this will save parentsâ€™ time for finding the most fit sitter
  • Messaging system between parents and caregivers

How to use our service?

  1. Sign up either as a parent user or sitter user.
  2. Browse! If you are a parent user, you can view a list of job-wanted posts. If you are a sitter user, there will be a list of job-openings waiting for you!
  3. When you find a post you like to apply or contact, sitter users can apply for a position and parent users can send a message to the sitter.
  4. Start to discuss the details of job with each other by messages!
